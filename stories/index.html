    Discover some of our Projects and meet the solution providers, social entrepreneurs and communities from around the world that are working to implement development initiatives in last-mile communities.

    Current Projects

    To learn more about our approach visit our Project Accelerator and Consulting pages.

    JOY Vermiculture

    Jaya Organic Yojana

    Launching farmer-led micro businesses to restore soil fertility and sequester carbon through earthworm composting in India.

    Her Health, Her Future

    Real Action for Community Empowerment

    Providing young women in Uganda with the tools and training to manage their menstrual health and stay in school.

    Tippy Taps Sierra Leone

    COMIN-SL

    Making hand washing convenient, accessible (and fun!) for communities in Sierra Leone.

    Sky Juice Villages

    The ANaRDe Foundation

    Leveraging ultrafiltration technology to provide cost-effective and sustainable access to clean water in rural villages across Gujarat, India.

    Energy for L'ENDPS

    Fondasyon Ludmilla Rousseau pour Haïti

    Connecting Haitian schools to the world via off-grid solutions.

    Light up Karnataka

    ESSMART Global

    Supporting women-owned businesses to distribute solar lanterns to off-grid families in Karnataka, India.

    Clean Water Communities

    Busoga Volunteers for Community Development

    Identifying market opportunities for expanding access to clean drinking water and sanitation facilities at the household level in Uganda.

    Water for Highland Livelihoods

    Alternative Indigenous Development Foundation

    Increasing water access for farmers and families in the highland communities of Iloilo in the Philippines.
