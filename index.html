The fight against poverty starts with satisfying basic human needs.

New solutions with the potential to dramatically improve lives at the "bottom of the pyramid" are emerging daily. TEL is an information nexus that connects innovators with solution seekers to lift individuals and communities out of poverty.

Contact Us
