Join the Team

Join our growing team of passionate individuals!

Interested in joining the TEL Team? Think you have a lot to contribute, but your time is limited? We do not currently have any full-time positions available; however, we are actively recruiting volunteers of all ages and backgrounds to grow with TEL. Even if you have only a few hours a month to spare, you can make a big impact with our organization. We also occasionally have openings from for paid internships.

If you are a student interested in an internship, we will work with your university for class credit.

How to Apply

If you are interested in joining the exciting and fast-paced non-profit sector, then please send your resume to contact@techxlab.org and let us know how you can contribute!

Volunteer Blogger / Content Creator

Location: Virtual Opportunity

Commitment: Part-time, flexible

Description: We are looking for a volunteer to create original content for our blog. This is a great opportunity for aspiring and established writers to see their work published on a high traffic website. We are interested in articles that address a wide spectrum of international development topics, including poverty innovations in the field, emerging tech for social good, and highlights of interesting people and organizations making a difference in the world.

This is a volunteer position. If you are a student interested in an internship, we will work with your university for class credit.
