    Program Director

    The Technology Exchange Lab, Inc. (TEL) is seeking an entrepreneurial, values-driven manager who can work independently in the context of a very small team to help spearhead our next stage of growth. Experience in nonprofit management and/or international development required. Responsibilities center on strategy implementation, field project direction, and financial development.

    You will take the lead in:

    • Development, implementation, and periodic updating of TEL’s strategic plan

    • Business development and program management

    • Team coordination

    • Partnership development and relationship management

    • Representing TEL as a spokesperson at conferences and events, occasionally outside of regular working hours

    Your qualifications:

    • A passion for implementing innovative, ecologically sound, technology-based approaches to improving the lives of people around the world.

    • At ease working to further the organization’s mission and strategy by actively engaging a variety of stakeholders, including community leaders, academics, c-suite executives, and leaders in the public sector.

    • Belief in teamwork and effective collaboration for meaningful results.

    • Familiarity with the International Development sector.

    • 2+ years of experience working with a nonprofit organization, social enterprise, start-up, or other experience relevant to TEL's mission.

    • Bachelor’s degree, MA preferred.

    • Excellent writing and oral communication skills in English, additional languages a plus.

    • Ability to travel internationally 2-4 weeks a year.

    • Creativity, self-motivation, and a sense of humor.

    This is a part-time position (20 hours/week), possibly leading to full-time, working at present remotely, later in Kendall Square, Cambridge, MA. Perks include membership to Cambridge Innovation Center, access to networking events, free daytime gym membership, snacks.

    To apply please send a cover letter, resume, and writing sample to contact@techxlab.org with the subject line: Program Director Application. Responses will only go out to candidates who have been selected for an interview. Interviews will be arranged on a rolling basis. We encourage you to submit your application as soon as possible.
