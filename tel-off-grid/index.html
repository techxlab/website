    TEL Off-Grid

    Our solutions database is now available through the TEL Off-Grid mobile app!

    TEL Off-Grid is available to download for free on Android phones from the Google Play store, with an iOS version for iPhones under development.

    With TEL Off-Grid, anyone can easily download information on over 600 solutions to problems of poverty on their smartphone, for offline browsing and sharing in the field.

    You can also favorite your preferred solutions for easy reference!
