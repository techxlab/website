Solution Overview & Benefits

The Potakit+ Basic Portable Water Quality Laboratory is a portable testing laboratory designed to assess the suitability of drinking water on site, even in remote areas. The Potakit+ tests for the following microbiological and physicochemical parameters:

-Fecal coliforms -Total coliforms -pH -Conductivity -Arsenic -Fluoride -Ammonia -Nitrite -Nitrate -Free Chlorine -Total Chlorine

History & Development

This kit is used by NGOs and water technicians implementing rural water and sanitation programs.

This kit was previously sold by Wagtech as the Potakit: Intermediate Low-Budget Testing Kit (physicochemical + microbiological). It is now available through Palintest as the updated Potakit+. Existing users of the Potakit can access consumables, spares and technical support from Palintest.
