MoonLight

Kamworks

Solution Overview & Benefits

The MoonLight is an effective light source for use in the rural areas of Cambodia where there is little access to electricity. An average day's worth of sunlight can provide a low intensity glow for use throughout the entire night, or a high intensity glow for around 4 hours. A 3 meter cord allows the attached solar panel to collect light outside while the lamp stays inside, preventing theft. The MoonLight can be used to provide lighting while walking or riding a bicycle, to promote community safety at night, and while doing business or studying at night. A yellow cord attached to the lights allows the user to even wear the MoonLight.

History & Development

Kamworks and team Lumen designed the MoonLight with cooperation from rural villagers living outside of Phnom Penh. The design team protyped four products, eventually selecting the MoonLight. The bright LEDs are durable and long-lasting, and any spare parts can be replaced locally. Delivery is now available in Phnom Penh. The MoonLight can be purchased for around $25USD or rented for around $0.08USD per day, eliminating the need to purchase kerosense which can lead to household fires and indoor air pollution. If purchased at full price, the MoonLight surpasses kerosene in equivalent value after about 8 months of average use.
