    Housing & Transport

    Poor housing and transportation limit a person’s ability to access basic human needs. By utilizing locally sourced materials and open source design, communities can satisfy both temporary and permanent housing and transportation needs. Improving these aspects of life is a catalyst for personal and community growth. Discover solutions for proper housing and transport that help ensure safety, security and health.
