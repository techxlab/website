Grifaid Family Aquafilter

The Safe Water Trust

Solution Overview & Benefits

The Grifaid Family Aquafilter® is a household water filter inspired by public water facilities technology, using hollow fibres/ultra-filtration membranes. It removes all biological pathogens down to 0.01 microns. The Grifaid Family Aquafilter® is designed to last a minimum of 5 years, requiring no spare parts, cartridges, or electricity. The Grifaid Family Aquafilter® produces safe drinking water on demand, at a rate of 90 liters per hour.

History & Development

Over the last 5 years, thousands of Grifaid Family Aquafilter® have been used in about 40 countries, including the DRC, Gambia, Ghana, The Philippines, Syria, Lebanon, OPT-Gaza, and Haiti. They have been distributed both by small charities and international NGOs, including MSF, Handicap International, OXFAM, Save the Children, Solidarites International, HelpAge, Muslim Aid, ACTED, CHF International, INTERSOS.
