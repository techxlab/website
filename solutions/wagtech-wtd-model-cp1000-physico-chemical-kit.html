Model CP1000 - Physico Chemical Kit

Wagtech WTD

Solution Overview & Benefits

The CP1000 Physico Chemical Kit is designed for testing a range of physical water quality parameters, and performing the analysis of over 40 different chemical parameters. The testing parameters include:

  • Conductivity
  • Turbidity
  • TDS
  • pH
  • Temperature
  • Arsenic
  • Photometer (40 chemical parameters)

The kit is best suited for field-based drilling engineers and groundwater testing applications. It is portable, waterproof and features pre-programmed test methods and touchscreen operation.

Wagtech CP1000 includes the following components:

  • 7100 advanced photometer
  • Conductivity calibration kit
  • Round test tubes (pack of 5)
  • Turbidity Standard
  • Test tube rack
  • Plastic bottles, autoclavable 60ml
  • Conductivity/TDS meter
  • Aluminum carrying case
  • pH/temp/mV meter
  • Training procedure manual
  • Portable turbidity meter
  • Full operating instructions
  • Calibration screwdrivers
  • Water quality report sheets
  • pH calibration kit
  • Wagtech digital arsenator

History & Development

Working in Northern Uganda with N.P.B.W. Drilling, a Kenyan drilling contractor, Wagtech endeavors to test water from drilled boreholes in the region. As micro-biological contamination is not generally an issue for water taken from deep underground, the testing concentrates on physiochemical parameters only. Using the CP1000 Physico Chemical Kit, a serious fluoride problem at one of the drilling sites was identified and steps were taken to rectify the problem, resulting in averting dangerous drinking water away from the main water supply.
