The Inyenyeri Fuel and Stove System

Inyenyeri

Solution Overview & Benefits

The mission of Inyenyeri's clean-cooking system is to eliminate death and disease caused by household air pollution in Rwanda. Inyenyeri offers high-tech stoves to customers that agree to buy their fuel pellets. Customers receive as many stoves as needed to replace all deadly stoves.

Low-Cost Fuel Pellets:

  • Comparable or lower cost fuel than cooking with charcoal
  • Cash-free fuel for rural households
  • Sustainably harvested feedstock
  • Long lasting: 1 kg burns ~1.5 hrs
  • Incredibly efficient: +80% less wood than charcoal
  • No messy soot
  • Locally produced and sold: consistent and convenient

The Philips Smokeless Fan-Gasifying Stove:

  • NO SMOKE: 98% less toxic and GHG emissions
  • Fan Gasifying: modern technology utilizing biomass energy
  • Easy and quick to light and extinguish
  • Clean fuel + flame: no black pots, clothing, walls, ceiling
  • Hot and adjustable flame : no tending or waiting
  • Durable, sleek, aspirational design

History & Development

The Philips Smokeless Fan-Gasifying Stove is a clean solid biomass stove: it meets IWA Tier 3 standards. Inyenyeri has identified potential improvements to achieve IWA Tier 4 Indoor Air Quality Standards - the emissions profile of LPG. They are currently working with several stove manufacturers to make this leap. Manufactured and imported from Lesotho, they optimize airflow, pot rests, and other features at their factory in Gisenyi to achieve increased efficiencies and emissions levels, calibrated to their specific pellet recipe.
