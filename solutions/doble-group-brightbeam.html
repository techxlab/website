BrightBeam

The Doble Group

Solution Overview & Benefits

BrightBeam was specifically designed for remote areas without access to electricity. BrightBeam is available in three models: BrightBeam Home, BrightBeam Home Plus, and BrightBeam Village Kit.

BrightBeam Home: Designed for use in homes, the BrightBeam Home range delivers up to 100 lumens of clean white light. Includes 5W portable solar panel.

  • 100 lumens for 9 hours – ‘High’ setting
  • 20 lumens for 40 hours – ‘Medium’ setting
  • 4 lumens for 200 hours – ‘Low’ setting

BrightBeam Home Plus: Designed to illuminate homes, schools and businesses, Doble’s BrightBeam Home Plus delivers up to 200 lumens of clean white light. Includes 10W portable solar panel.

  • 200 lumens for 9 hours – ‘High’ setting
  • 40 lumens for 40 hours – ‘Medium’ setting
  • 8 lumens for 200 hours – ‘Low’ setting

BrightBeam Village Kit: Designed to illuminate large areas with up to 400 lumens of clean white light, Doble’s BrightBeam Village Kit can be used for lighting schools, businesses, medical centres, markets and other community spaces. Includes two 10W portable solar panels.

  • 400 lumens for 9 hours – ‘High’ setting
  • 80 lumens for 40 hours – ‘Medium’ setting
  • 16 lumens for 200 hours – ‘Low’ setting

Each BrightBeam model also acts as a formidable power source, charging mobile phones, radios and other devices.

History & Development

BrightBeam came out of a need to provide affordable and effective off-grid solar lighting solutions for the developing world.
