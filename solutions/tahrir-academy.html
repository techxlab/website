Solution Overview & Benefits

Tahrir Academy is an Egyptian-based organization that provides a video catalogue that aims to crowdsource knowledge and be a non-traditional learning destination. The academy relies on an engaged community and volunteers from various backgrounds and age groups to design, review and deliver educational audio and visual content for users.

The Academy has published more than 100 educational videos on topics including physics, astronomy, grammar, and chemical experiments for elementary and secondary school students.
