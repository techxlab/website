Solution Overview & Benefits

Milaap is an online fund-raising platform that partners with proven, credible grass-roots level organizations (called ‘Field Partners’) to identify deserving borrowers, the amount of financial assistance they need and the intended purpose of the funds. Their network of volunteers works with field partners to edit and review loan profiles. Milaap is then able to link borrowers to people around the world who are willing to fund them.

History & Development

Milaap is a mission-driven organization changing the way people fund and impact communities in need. Every day, Milaap connects with hundreds of hardworking borrowers who are looking to start a small business, pay for education, install better facilities in their households, and more, with people around the world willing to lend.
