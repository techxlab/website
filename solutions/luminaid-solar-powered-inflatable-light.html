Solution Overview & Benefits

The LuminAID is a solar-powered LED light encased in an inflatable plastic case. Once charged in the sun for 6-7 hours, the LuminAID provides up to 15 hours of LED light, and is able to hold a full charge for over 4 months. Its inflatable case makes the LuminAID waterproof, while also reducing glare.

The LuminAID can be packed flat, allowing for the storage and shipment of up to 50 units, compared to 8 units of a small conventional flashlight, by volume. Designed for long-term use, the LuminAID can withstand hundreds of recharge cycles, and multiple years of use.

History & Development

Founded and developed by two graduate students studying architecture and design, the LuminAID was developed in response to the basic need for light in post-natural disaster situations following the 2010 earthquake in Haiti. The LuminAID light can be purchased online at USD $19.95 per-unit. LuminAID also operates a one-for-one business model, in which individuals can purchase one unit and donate one unit for USD $27.95, or simply donate one unit for USD $10.00.

While LuminAID lights are currently only available for online purchase and distribution in the U.S., LuminAID has distributed over 5000 lights across projects in Haiti, Ghana, India and the Philippines.
