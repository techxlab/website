Envirofit Wood Stoves

Envirofit International

Solution Overview & Benefits

Envirofit wood stoves are a tested biomass cooking solution with multiple different models available:

  • M-5000 Wood
  • G-3300 Wood
  • B-1200 Wood
  • Econofire Wood
  • Powerflame
  • Z-3000 Built-In
  • HM-5000 Plancha

Envirofit wood stoves are designed with the following features:

  • Reduces smoke, harmful gasses, and toxic emissions
  • Reduces biomass fuel use
  • Reduces cooking time
  • Easy to use, clean and maintain

History & Development

Before a new cookstove product is ready for high volume production, we subject our prototypes to rigorous stress testing that simulates multiple lifetimes of use. We want to be the ones that find and fix problems, not our customers. To meet this challenge, we partnered with Colorado State University to create the most advanced cookstove emissions testing laboratory in the world.
