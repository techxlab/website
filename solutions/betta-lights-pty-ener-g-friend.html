Solution Overview & Benefits

The ener-G-friend is an off-grid power pack solution designed to supply energy to laptops, tablets, mobile phones, inkjet printers and other devices such as point of sale electronics, cameras and photo printers to be used in the field or in small, off-grid offices or offices with unreliable power sources.

The ener-G-friend’s battery pack is charged either from a solar panel or from a 220 Volt AC charger which is available as an optional item from Betta Lights.

The ener-G-friend is available in the OFFICE and TRAVELLER models, and single or 4 port USB tablet computer versions. Project specific models can be configured with the available building blocks.

Applications:

  • Off-grid rural and small offices
  • E-Schooling projects
  • Point of sale devices
  • Configurable to incorporate an AC charger to use the solution as a redundant power supply, should the grid power fail for critical computer devices
