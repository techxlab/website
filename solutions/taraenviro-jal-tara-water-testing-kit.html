Jal-TARA Water Testing Kit

TARAEnviro

Solution Overview & Benefits

The Standard Jal-TARA Kit is designed to perform basic tests to ensure water potability. The kit can test for the following 11 physical, chemical and biological parameters:

-pH -Temperature -Turbidity -Hardness -Chloride -Fluoride -Iron -Nitrate -Residual Chlorine -Ammonia -Coliform Bacteria

History & Development

TARAenviro markets water testing and water purification solutions under the Jal-TARA brand. These products test the potability of water and offer cost-effective solutions for purifying water. All Jal-TARA products are designed for individuals, NGOs, students and public authorities to actively participate in monitoring water quality and to protect scarce fresh water sources from further pollution.
