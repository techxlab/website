Solution Overview & Benefits

"MAGH 3G" is the most adoptable stove, for anyone and anywhere. All types of biomass (sticks / chips of wood / wood shavings / leaves, etc), pellets, briquettes, cow dung cakes and charcoal can be used for cooking. This is an all in one stove. It was found that many families have at least two or three types of stoves in rural areas for using types of biomass as fuel. Now with just one stove they have the freedom to use all types of Biomass as fuel. There is an option to control primary air, to control air from the fuel feed side opening, and secondary air (while using TLUD adopter).

Easy to transport, efficiencies from 25% to 40% based on the type of fuel and mode of operation, easy to cook in open air conditions, transportation cost is less, can meet the cooking needs of 5 to 10 people, all types of food can be cooked, less time required to train people on how to operate it.

History & Development

Low-cost, efficient Good Stoves were designed by Dr. N. Sai Bhaskar Reddy, Founder and CEO of Geoecology Energy Organisation (GEO) for people using biomass as fuel. All the innovative designs of GEO are declared as OHANDA / Open Knowledge / Creative Commons for communities to adopt / adapt.
