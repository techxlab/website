    Solution Overview & Benefits

    SOLARKIOSK's E-HUBB is a versatile modular hub that can cater to any type of business or activity. The SOLARKIOSK Power Core integrates solar power technology to convert sunlight into direct current electricity and make it available for productive use and integrated appliances, such as refrigerators, TVs, internet, charging stations, etc. Even after dusk, or during rainy seasons, all appliances continue to function due to the electricity which is stored in the power bank. is a highly optimized kiosk with solar panels on top. Once installed, it becomes a compact, affordable and sturdy shop offering energy, products, tools and services.

    The E-HUBB’s technical design has an open interface and is therefore able to power almost every standard AC or DC electrical appliance. For example: refrigerator, computer, TV, POS system, in-store lighting, or mobile phone charger, 24/7, copier/printer/scanner, entertainment (music). The modularity and expandability of the Power Core systems allow power upgrades in order to ensure sufficient, reliable power capacity to run nearly any type of appliance. The E-HUBB is ready for integration of other equipment such as ATMs and POS system terminals that can power a vast variety of electrical appliances.

    All E-HUBBs have construction details as described below and come as a turnkey solution. They include, depending on their specification, the following electrical appliances which come with the E-HUBB so businesses can start their operations immediately:

    The E-HUBB can be equipped with flexible shelf/storage space to store and showcase products. The E-HUBB creates an inviting shopping environment through its large counter space and easy to open hatch, increasing foot traffic. The secure and durable steel shell offers protection against break-ins and guarantees a long life with minimized maintenance costs. The SOLARKIOSK designed and engineered solar rooftop mounting system ensures that the solar panels are safely mounted and well-secured against theft. The SOLARKIOSK power core system ensures that the E-HUBB works 24/7. Standard E-HUBBs are available with sizes from 0,5 – 2,3 kWp. Customized E-HUBBs can go up to 40 kWp. The power core system of the E-HUBB has a state-of-the-art battery management and protection system and uses only high quality components to ensure as little maintenance as possible and long life of the electrical system.
