Solution Overview & Benefits

The Atom, a bicycle generator and rechargeable battery pack, uses the current created by the wheel's rotation to provide a USB compatible output of 5V at roughly the same rate as the USB output from a computer.

The Atom mounts on standard-sized bicycles. To do so, remove the quick release or wheel nut on the back wheel, attach the Atom generator to the back wheel, and lastly replace the wheel/ Atom combo back onto the frame.

History & Development

Siva Cycle is ready to move to full production and are hoping to begin shipping generators by November 2013.
