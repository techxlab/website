    Solution Overview & Benefits

    TELESIS is an evidence-based innovation utilising validated and/or tried-and-tested technology. It is a superior progression of precursor versions implemented in the Arabian / Persian Gulf by the Founder of Composium Group Ltd.

    The ultimate objective of our research is to establish and validate the potential of sub surface water as a renewable (virtually inexhaustible) feed source that will significantly transform and secure all of the following utilities and facilities, which are either individually or in synergistic combinations where the overall effect is greater than the sum of the separate utilities. This notion includes the following: water production, energy production, district heating & cooling, and Horticultural and Aqua cultural operations – serving the Food-Water-Energy Nexus while resolving the Security, Affordability, and Sustainability Trilemma.

    All TELESIS processes are incomparably economic, ceaselessly sustainable, and environmentally benign. Desalination production costs are ≥50% lower than the next best option. The intrinsic energy of the abstracted waters serves to generate electricity that can reduce the desalination production costs further still [≥80% lower] with ZERO carbon emissions desalination or can be engaged in other revenue streams such as Greenhouse Agriculture.

    From the UN Sustainable Development Goals, TELESIS significantly assists with resolving 11 of the 17 SDG’s.

    History & Development

    Our company founder has practitioner experience of precursor versions of TELESIS. The problem with these precursor version was primarily the eye-watering cost. Ron Daniel has since spent many years researching, developing, and validating a superior version that costs considerably less. The biggest economy stems from the fact that TELESIS uniquely offer at least two utilities for less than the price of one.
