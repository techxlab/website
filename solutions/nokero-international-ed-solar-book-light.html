Solution Overview & Benefits

The N50 Ed Book Light is a solar powered, dust proof and water proof LED light. It provides light allowing people to read, study, and learn by night or in dark places. The N50 is made from child-friendly materials and has a flexible gooseneck with a LED light attached to the end. It clips easily onto books, homework packets, or sits on a flat surface to light a workspace. It can provide up to 3 hours of light if properly charged.
