Solution Overview & Benefits

The Ecocina is an affordable, portable and fuel-efficient clean cookstove that reduces wood consumption and harmful pollutants.

Ecocina Stoves:

  • Reduce burns, respiratory, eye and skin problems
  • Reduce carbon emissions by 68% and particulate matter by 86%
  • Reduce wood use by over 50%
  • Are portable and quick-cooking, requiring no installation or chimney
  • Can be built from all local materials such as concrete, pumice and tile
  • Provide local jobs in sustainable factories
  • Are adapted to local cooking methods

The Ecocina can be made using locally sourced materials.
