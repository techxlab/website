Implementing Clean-Delivery Kit distribution programs to ensure access to adequate birthing environments.

St. Boniface Foundation is a nonprofit health clinic that serves rural Haitian communities through holistic health programs, particularly focusing on maternal and neonatal health.

St. Boniface Foundation is seeking Clean Delivery Kit Providers to assemble specialized kits that are culturally appropriate for rural Haitian users. We require kits that satisfy the following requirements:

  • Sterilized equipment
  • Easy-to-use instructions for non-literate users
  • Low cost (under $4 per-kit)
  • Easily delivered to Haiti, or able to assemble on site

St. Boniface Foundation is implementing a clean-delivery kit distribution (CDK) program within three districts in Haiti. Our community health workers will educate midwives and traditional birthing attendants regarding the importance and use of clean delivery kits. They will in turn provide CDKs to expecting mothers, and instruct mothers on how to properly use kits. The pilot project will take place within one Haitian community, with the possibility for a scaled-up project throughout other regions.
