Solar Program Karnataka

Posted by TEL Team 10 December 2015

The Project

The Solar Program Karnataka is an initiative of Sumangali Seva Ashrama to power 30,000 rural homes with point-of-use solar light solutions. By outfitting remote mom & pop shops with inventories of solar lights through a microconsignment model, the program creates employment opportunities for local entrepreneurs, while also providing a safe, cost-effective and sustainable alternative to kerosene lamps for thousands of families.

TEL's Role

Upon engaging the organization through a detailed process of needs assessment, TEL assisted the program in identifying and procuring solar lighting solutions from five different providers. TEL also applied to joint grant opportunities with Sumangali Seva Ashrama for funds to aid in the procurement of solar lights.

Results

After field testing the five demo units and conducting extensive interviews with stakeholders, the Solar Program Karnataka is in the process of drafting an MOU with one of the providers. The program has also received detailed applications form 30,000 qualified households eager to participate.
